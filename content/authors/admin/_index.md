I have been in Cork, Ireland since January 2020, first as an Irish Research Council Government of Ireland post-doctoral fellow at APC Microbiome Ireland, then as a lecturer at the Department of Pharmacology & Therapeutics, UCC. Since October 2024, I hold a permanent lectureship in Pharmacology and Therapeutics at UCC.

I am also a senior scientist at APC Microbiome Ireland and co-chair the UCC Race Equality Network. I serve as a director in the board of the ALBA Network and as the Science Communication Coordinator of the EU COST Action Affect-Evo.
